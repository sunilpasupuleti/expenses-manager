Expenses Aura is your all-in-one personal finance tracker, designed to help you manage spending effortlessly with a clean, intuitive interface and powerful features — all while keeping your data fully private and secure.

Feature Highlights

Bank Account Integration
Securely link your supported bank accounts to fetch real-time balances and transaction history — no manual entry needed.

Data Encryption
Your data is stored securely and encrypted on your device. No one else — not even us — can access it.

Smart Scanning
Scan your receipts or bills directly using the camera and automatically categorize transactions with date and category detection.

Easy Exporting
Export your data in multiple formats (PDF, Excel, JSON) to use it however and wherever you like.

Import from JSON
Restore your complete accounts and transactions from a previously exported JSON backup — perfect for device changes or app reinstalls.

Multiple Account Support
Create and manage separate accounts with individual currencies and track each account’s transactions independently.

Dashboard and Analytics
View powerful category-wise breakdowns and percentages, with dynamic pie charts and dashboards for each account.

Trends Overview
Visualize your financial trends over the past 14 days and 12 months with interactive line and bar charts.

Account Options
Pin, archive, edit, or delete any account with a simple tap.

Transaction Attachments
Attach images to any transaction for easy future reference.

Live Currency Rates
Automatically updated exchange rates help you manage foreign currency accounts with ease.

Daily Backup and History
Your data is backed up daily at midnight, with restore access to your last 10 backups — automated and worry-free.

EMI and Loan Management
Add flexible loan accounts with amortization schedules and EMI tracking built-in.

Smart Reminders
Set daily notification reminders to log missed expenses on time.

App Lock
Protect your financial data with a secure app lock.

Dark Mode and Quick Actions
Enjoy a modern UI with dark mode, swipeable actions, quick input, and context menus that streamline your workflow.

Expenses Aura is now faster, smarter, and fully encrypted. Whether you’re budgeting, tracking a loan, or reviewing your spending trends, manage everything in one secure place.
